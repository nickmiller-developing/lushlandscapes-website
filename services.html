Common questions about our services

Get quick answers before reaching out

Do you offer one-time or recurring service? ▾
Both! We do one-time projects like patios and garden installations, and we also offer recurring maintenance plans for lawn care, seasonal cleanups, and ongoing garden upkeep.
How do I get a quote? ▾
Fill in our contact form or call us directly. We'll schedule a free on-site visit to assess your space and provide a detailed quote — no obligation.
How long does a typical project take? ▾
It depends on the scope. A standard lawn cleanup or planting job might take a day. A full patio or fencing project typically runs 2–5 days. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.
Are your materials eco-friendly? ▾
Where possible, yes. We use low-impact fertilizers, organic mulch, and sustainable planting choices. We're happy to discuss eco-friendly options for your project specifically.
