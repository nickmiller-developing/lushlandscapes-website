About GreenScape

A local landscaping company built on passion, reliability, and a love for the outdoors.

Our Story

GreenScape was founded over 12 years ago with a simple belief: that every property deserves to look its best. What started as a small lawn care operation has grown into a full-service landscaping company serving hundreds of residential and commercial clients across the region.

We take pride in our work, show up on time, and treat every property as if it were our own. Our team of experienced landscapers, designers, and hardscaping specialists bring skill and dedication to every project — big or small.
