Who we are

Built on a love of the outdoors

GreenScape has been transforming properties across Cambridge, Ontario for over 12 years. We're a local team that takes real pride in every project.

A local business, rooted in the community

GreenScape started as a small lawn care operation over a decade ago. What began with a few residential clients and a single truck has grown into a full-service landscaping team serving hundreds of homes and businesses across Cambridge and the wider region.

We've grown because of one thing: doing the work properly and treating every client with honesty. We show up when we say we will, communicate clearly, and we don't stop until the job looks right.

Every member of our team shares the same commitment to quality — whether it's a quick hedge trim or a full hardscaping installation.

Do you work year-round? ▾
We operate through all seasons in Cambridge. Spring and fall are ideal for most projects, but we handle winter maintenance and summer work as needed.

Yes. GreenScape is fully licensed and insured. We carry liability coverage and all our team members are trained professionals.
