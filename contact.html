Common questions

Quick answers before you reach out

How quickly will you respond? ▾
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent requests, please call us directly at (519) 123-4567.
Is the quote really free? ▾
Yes, completely free and with no obligation. We'll schedule an on-site visit to see your space and give you a detailed estimate before any work begins.
What areas do you service? ▾
We service Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo, and surrounding areas. Not sure if we cover your location? Just reach out and we'll confirm.
Do I need to be home for the quote? ▾
For outdoor assessments we can often complete an initial look without you present. We'll always confirm details with you before any work starts.
Can you handle emergency repairs? ▾
Yes. Storm damage, fallen trees, or urgent fence repairs can often be scheduled quickly. Call us directly and we'll work to fit you in as soon as possible.
