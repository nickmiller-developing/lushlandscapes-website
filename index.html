FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our work and process

Do you work year-round? ▾
We operate through all seasons in Cambridge. Spring and fall are ideal for most projects, but we handle winter maintenance and summer work as needed. Contact us to discuss timing for your specific project.
What areas do you service? ▾
GreenScape serves Cambridge and the surrounding region. We handle residential and property work throughout the area. Reach out to confirm we cover your location.
How do you price projects? ▾
We provide quotes based on the scope of work, materials needed, and labor involved. Every project is unique, so we assess your space and needs before giving an estimate.
Can you handle emergency repairs? ▾
Yes. Storm damage, fallen trees, or urgent fence repairs can often be scheduled quickly. Call us to discuss what needs attention and we'll work to fit you in.
Do you offer maintenance plans? ▾
We do. Ongoing lawn care, garden maintenance, and seasonal work keep your landscape healthy. We can set up a schedule that works for your needs and budget.
